## Logo Variants

### Standard Logo (horizontal, black background)
- **URL:** `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jalenjjbr/ellery-assets/refs/heads/main/ellery_logo_1.png`
- **Description:** Full "ellerypartners" wordmark with infinity-loop icon. Blue "ellery" + grey "partners" on black background.
- **Use when:** Default logo placement on dark backgrounds, hero sections, title slides, formal documents. This is the primary logo.

### Short Logo (mark only, black background)
- **URL:** `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jalenjjbr/ellery-assets/refs/heads/main/ellery_logo_short_3.png`
- **Description:** Abbreviated logo showing infinity icon + "ellery" only (no "partners"). Blue text on black background.

### Logo — Black & White (white background)
- **URL:** `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jalenjjbr/ellery-assets/refs/heads/main/ellery_logo_bw.png`
- **Description:** Full wordmark in grey/dark tones on black background. No blue; monochrome treatment.
- **Use when:** Grayscale or single-color contexts, printed materials where color isn't available, watermarks, formal legal documents.

### Logo — Blue + White Text (black background)
- **URL:** `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jalenjjbr/ellery-assets/refs/heads/main/ellery_logo_white_2.png`
- **Description:** Full wordmark with blue "ellery" and white "partners" on black background. White infinity icon.
- **Use when:** Dark-mode or gradient backgrounds where the grey "partners" text in the standard logo wouldn't have enough contrast.

### Logo — Stacked Right (black background)
- **URL:** `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jalenjjbr/ellery-assets/refs/heads/main/ellery_stacked_right.png`
- **Description:** Vertical/stacked layout with infinity icon top-right, "ellery" and "partners" stacked below. Blue + grey on black.
- **Use when:** Tall/vertical spaces, square-format placements, slide corners, letterhead, presentation cover art where horizontal space is limited.

## Preferred Assets (Default Choices)

When in doubt, reach for these first:

- **Logo:** Standard Logo (`ellery_logo_1.png`) for most contexts; Stacked Right (`ellery_stacked_right.png`) when vertical/square format is needed
- **Headshots:** Arms Crossed versions for both Orlando and Jalen (use circle crops only for small avatar/profile contexts)
- **Banner:** Header/Footer Banner (`header-footer.jpg`) for email and document headers

Other variants exist for specific situations (B&W for legal, tiles for social profiles, etc.) but the three above cover 90% of use cases.

## Quick Reference: Choosing the Right Asset

| Context | Recommended Asset |
|---------|-------------------|
| Slide deck title page | Standard Logo ⭐ or Blue+White Logo on gradient background |
| Slide deck header bar | Header/Footer Banner |
| Slide deck team/bio page | Arms Crossed headshots ⭐ + Standard Logo |
| Dark background / gradient | Blue+White Logo or Standard Logo ⭐ |
| Light / white background | B&W Logo or use Standard Logo on a dark card |
| Square social profile | Icon Tile — Blue |
| Email signature / footer | Header/Footer Banner or Short Logo + Circle Crop headshots |
| Favicon / small icon | Icon Tile — Blue or Infinity Icon |
| Vertical / tall space | Stacked Right Logo ⭐ |
| Watermark / decorative | Infinity Icon (medium) |
| Formal / legal documents | B&W Logo |
| Team page / About Us | Arms Crossed headshots ⭐ |
| Avatars / small circular | Circle Crop headshots |

⭐ = preferred default
